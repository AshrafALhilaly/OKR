### Top 5 Risks & Mitigations

#### 1. **Competitor Response** (HIGH RISK)

**Risk**: Notion or Airtable adds AI column filling within 6 months

**Probability**: 70%  
**Impact**: High (market share loss)

**Mitigation**:
- Move fast: Launch within 12 weeks

- Build template marketplace (network effects)
- Add collaboration features (hard for competitors)

**Success Metric**: Establish 30% adoption before competitor ships

---

#### 2. **User Adoption Barrier** (MEDIUM RISK)

**Risk**: Users don't discover or understand columns feature

**Probability**: 50%  
**Impact**: High (feature flop)

**Mitigation**:
- In-app tooltip on first notebook open
- Templated column sets (Literature Review, Competitive Analysis)
- 90-second tutorial video
- Progressive disclosure (hide advanced features initially)

**Success Metric**: 50% of exposed users try feature within 7 days

---

#### 3. **AI Quality Issues** (MEDIUM RISK)

**Risk**: AI-generated column content is inaccurate or irrelevant

**Probability**: 40%  
**Impact**: Medium (user dissatisfaction)

**Mitigation**:
- Source attribution in every cell ("Generated from [Source]")
- Confidence indicators (High/Medium/Low)
- Edit affordance (clear visual cue)
- A/B test 3 prompt templates per use case

**Success Metric**: 70% user satisfaction (thumbs up/down)

---

#### 4. **Performance Degradation** (LOW RISK)

**Risk**: AI column filling too slow with 50+ rows

**Probability**: 30%  
**Impact**: Medium (poor UX)

**Mitigation**:
- Progressive processing UI ("Filling row 1 of 50...")
- Batching (10 rows at a time, 2-second delays)
- Rate limiting (10 operations per hour per user)
- Cancel button for long operations

**Success Metric**: <2% AI operation failure rate

---

#### 5. **Legal/Compliance** (LOW RISK)

**Risk**: AI-generated content raises liability concerns

**Probability**: 20%  
**Impact**: Very High (launch blocker)

**Mitigation**:
- Disclaimer: "AI-generated content, verify accuracy"
- User owns and controls all data
- No automated decisions without human review
- Legal review completed before beta

**Success Metric**: Legal sign-off by Week 2
